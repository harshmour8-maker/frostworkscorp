About On The Rocks

On The Rocks is built to redefine how packaged ice is produced, stored, and delivered. Our focus is simple — deliver crystal‑clear, food‑safe premium ice cubes with consistent quality every single time.

Designed for homes, parties, cafes, and bars, our production process follows strict hygiene and filtration standards to ensure purity in every cube. From manufacturing to last‑mile delivery, we prioritize reliability, temperature integrity, and operational efficiency.

We are currently operating in Guwahati and Siliguri and expanding steadily through quick‑commerce platforms.
