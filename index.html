About On The Rocks

On The Rocks was created to bring reliability, hygiene, and premium quality to an everyday product that is often overlooked — ice. By combining structured production systems with FMCG‑style operational discipline, the brand ensures consistency across manufacturing, storage, and delivery.

Every cube is produced using multi‑stage purified water and handled in temperature‑controlled environments to maintain clarity, density, and safety. The result is slow‑melting, crystal‑clear ice designed for both home consumption and commercial use.

The company is currently serving customers in Guwahati and Siliguri through direct distribution and quick‑commerce partnerships while steadily expanding its production and logistics network.
